Emily’s Story

Site created on May 19, 2009

Hello there, I created this page for Emily so everyone can see her progress in recovering, feel free to leave her comments, pictures, and messages....I will try and keep this as updated as I can...Thank you,

Dustin


On April 28th, 2009 Emily was in a tragic car accident, she broke her back, most of her ribs, her lungs collapsed, and she sustained some head trauma...She was flown to Bronson Hospital for immeadiate attention and care....She spent the first several nights in unstable condition, her spinal cord was severed at the T-11, she had spinal surgery 8 days later and had to have 2 rods and 14 screws put in to repair her spine. She woke up two days later and has since continued to surprise all of us with how strong she is.

Thank you to the person who found her, Thank you to everyone who was at the scene, Thank you to every paramedic, doctor, and nurse who has helped her, Thank you to all of her family and friends for being so supportive...She is continuing to surprise everyone in her remarkable recovery so far
